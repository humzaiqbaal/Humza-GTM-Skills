# LinkedIn Graphic Design Brief Templates

Structured templates for creating infographics, carousels, and animated visuals to accompany LinkedIn posts.

---

## Infographic Brief Template

```markdown
# Graphic Design Brief: [Title]

## Content Type
[Single infographic / Carousel / Animated infographic]

## Dimensions
- LinkedIn feed: 1050 x 1380px (portrait, max feed real estate)
- Carousel: 1080 x 1350px per slide
- Square: 1080 x 1080px

## Header Section
[Title text — large, bold]
[Subtitle — lighter weight]
[Author name / company branding — top-right]

## Content Sections
### Section 1: [Name]
**Layout:** [left/right/full-width]
**Content:**
- [Bullet points of what goes in this section]
- [Include specific numbers, tool names, metrics]

### Section 2: [Name]
[Same structure]

## Visual Elements
- Tool logos to include: [list]
- Icons needed: [list]
- Flow diagram / arrows / connectors: [describe]

## Design Notes
- [Specific callouts for the designer]
- [Emphasis points — what should be BIG]
- [Typography hierarchy]
```

---

## Carousel Brief Template

```markdown
# Carousel Brief: [Title]

## Slide Count: [X slides]

## Cover Slide (SLIDE 1)
**Hook text:** [Large, bold statement]
**Subtext:** [Supporting line]
**Visual:** [What the cover should look like]
**Bottom:** "Swipe for the framework →"

## SLIDE 2: [Title]
**Layout:** [Split, full-width, comparison, etc.]
**Content:** [What goes on this slide]
**Key stat to highlight:** [The big number]
**Design notes:** [Colors, emphasis]

## SLIDE 3-N: [Continue pattern]

## Final Slide (CTA)
**Text:** [CTA copy]
**Elements:** [Profile photo, logo, website]
```

---

## Animated Infographic Notes

- Speed up to 1.5-2x for LinkedIn autoplay
- Loop seamlessly (end state = start state)
- Convert to GIF for audio-less content (outperforms video)
- Keep under 15 seconds for max loops in feed
- Big title visible in first frame (acts as second hook)

---

## Format Selection Guide

| Content Type | Best Visual | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Tech stack / system architecture | Single infographic (1050x1380) | Dense data, all in one view |
| Step-by-step / multi-topic | Carousel (1080x1350/slide) | Natural slide progression |
| Maximum feed real estate | Animated infographic | Movement catches eye, loops in feed |
| Before/after / tool battles | Comparison graphic | Side-by-side layout |
| Personal stories / hot takes | Text-only | Let the story breathe |

---

## Carousel Best Practices

- **8-12 slides** is the optimal range
- Cover slide = second hook (must stop the scroll on its own)
- Large text readable on mobile (16pt+ minimum)
- One idea per slide — never crowd slides
- Consistent design language across all slides
- CTA on last slide (follow, comment, visit profile)
- "Swipe →" indicator on cover and early slides

---

## Infographic Best Practices

- Big title visible = acts as a second hook in the feed
- Key stat should be the largest text element
- Use white/card-based sections for readability
- Include tool logos for instant recognition
- Flow diagrams with arrows for process visualization
- Reference past infographics for style consistency
